# Punctuation (hyphen vs em dash)

Do **not** use the Unicode em dash character (U+2014). Use the ASCII hyphen-minus `-` instead.

- For breaks or appositions, use spaces around the hyphen: `-` (e.g. `Scope - PASS`, `retry - do not stop`).
- In titles or tight labels, a single `-` without spaces is fine (e.g. `TC-1: Title - PASS`).

This applies to Testing Summaries, Jira comments, and other user-facing text in this workspace.
